This page is home to some of my favorite places on the internet. Catalogues of old radio shows, wax cylinder recordings, and curious creations made by people other than myself. I hope you enjoy these as much as I do.

## Favorite Finds

- ["Attention K-Mart Shoppers..."](https://archive.org/details/attentionkmartshoppers)
- [UCSB Wax Cylinder Archive](https://cylinders.library.ucsb.edu/)
- ["Old-Time" Radio Shows](https://archive.org/details/oldtimeradio)
- [Living Tree Furniture](https://www.livingtreefurniture.com/)
- [The VOID](https://www.thevoid.com/)
- [Unrealized Experimental Prototype City of Tomorrow](https://epcot.adrianmann.uk/)

I highly recommend *Dragnet* (crime mysteries based on real LAPD cases, 1930s-1950s), *X Minus One* (the sci-fi radio show that inspired many iconic stories), and *Let George Do It* (a fictional mystery series that starts out corny and gets much darker as the series progresses).
